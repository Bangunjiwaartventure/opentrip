About Mapur Island

Pulau Mapur merupakan salah satu pulau indah yang terletak di Provinsi Kepulauan Riau. Pulau ini berada di wilayah Kecamatan Bintan Pesisir, Kabupaten Bintan. Dengan jarak lokasi sekitar 21 kilometer dari Kota Kijang tidak terlalu jauh. Pulau ini menjadi pusat destinasi yang terkenal di Bintan yang wajib dikunjungi.

A Tropical Paradise

Bintan Island spans 1,140 square kilometers and is the largest island in the Riau Archipelago. Known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and lush tropical landscapes, it has become a favorite destination for travelers seeking both relaxation and adventure.

The island's strategic location has made it a cultural melting pot, where Malay, Chinese, and Indonesian influences blend harmoniously, creating a unique and vibrant local culture.

Top Attractions

Trikora Beach

A pristine 16-kilometer stretch of white sand beach perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports. Crystal clear waters and gentle waves make it ideal for families.

Perfect for relaxation

Mangrove Discovery

Explore the mystical mangrove forests by boat and discover unique ecosystems. Spot exotic birds, monitor lizards, and learn about conservation efforts.

Eco-adventure

Cultural Villages

Visit traditional Malay villages and experience authentic local culture. Witness traditional crafts, taste local cuisine, and learn about island heritage.

Cultural immersion
